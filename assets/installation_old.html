Symbiotic Currents (2023)
Interactive installation trough video projection.
My involvement is mainly on the interaction and projection done with touchdesigner.

Symbiotic Currents is an interactive installation that symbolises the connection between energy networks in nature and in technology.
It highlights the role of humans in bridging the gap and facilitating the harmony between the two.
The installation embraces an optimistic view of the future, and invites its audience to participate in the creation of that future.

In collabaration with: Lucas Dekker, Silke Hoekstra and Yael Cohen



Commencement (2022)
Interactive film art through video projection.
My involvement is mainly on the visuals made with after effects.


The anthropogenic effect is the consequences of human behavior on nature.
We have created a biosphere that represents this accelerated process.
The public will be greeted upon entering with a soothing projection of leaf branches.
Sound from the public will change the biosphere.
Nature can heal, but it can never be returned to its original state. But what happens if you go too far?

In collabaration with: Lucas Dekker, Ron Smits and Dewi Mac Donald

Redetwisten (2023)
Extended reality experience through VR, video projection and light.
My involvement is mainly on the animation made in after effects and directing videos trough touchdesigner.


Fire kept us warm and safe. Fire was there for us. How could fire break our trust? How could fire destroy everything as though it was nothing? To this day, opinions on the fate of the fire are divided. Do we keep the fire with us, despite all the dangers it brings, or do we take away all its powers and extinguish it forever? Experience two sides of the story and then make the decision.

In collaboration with: Anton Vermeer, Joni van de Griendt, Robin Gaanderse and Sire mane



Soy for people, not livestock (2023)
Installation trough monitors and video projection.
Made with after effects and isadora


I want to present a clear, easy to understand picture of the soy industry, and the problems this industry brings.
Soy is a good example of how incomprehensible and unethical the global food industry can be.
From deforestation to feeding soy to cattle and all the travel it takes for a piece of meat to end up on someone’s plate.

Voice by: Noor Mohamed
